Participant Guide

Quick Guide

Welcome to Hack OHI/O! Be sure to follow our rules posted [here]. Find more information about how you will be judged [here].
All teams must register to be judged by 10:00 AM at https://go.osu.edu/hackteam19

How Participants are Judged

Every team is judged by 4 judges for general prizes. If you opt to compete in a sponsor challenge, you will also be judged by the sponsors
The top 10 teams overall plus the sponsor challenge winners will be declared "Finalists of HackOHI/O 2020" Finalists will give a 3 minute final demo at the Sunday showcase. A VIP panel of judges will listen to the top teams and then the final awards will be presented.
